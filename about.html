COMPANY OVERVIEW

UBUNTU ENGINEERING CC is a well-established construction and engineering consultation film established in 2018 with the aim to serve the nation professionally in the field of Civil Engineering, contract administration & supervision, Mechanical Engineering and Electrical Engineering. We are a team of smart, multi-disciplinary professionals working together in a culture of continuous improvement. Never settling on status quo, we offer a fresh perspective on engineering solutions.

Ubuntu Engineering cc is a full-service construction, contracting and consultation firm founded in Windhoek, Namibia by young black Namibian individuals. Ubuntu Engineering cc intents to empower and motivate young Namibian engineers, especially those from previously disadvantage backgrounds to take ownership of the engineering industry, thereby making contribution to poverty and unemployment eradication through job creation and ultimately contributing to the socio-economic development of the country.

CLIENTS

To provide a professional service of the highest standard that meets the client’s requirements and project’s specifications with regard to costs, duration and management.

COMMUNITY

To undertake projects in cooperation with communities in order to meet the needs and aspirations, to provide employment opportunities which contribute towards the overall benefit of the country.

ENVIRONMENT

To design and manage the implementation of projects in such a manner as to minimise any pollution, harm to the environment, or ecological disturbance; furthermore, to strictly adhere to the regulations of relevant authorities. Staff To maintain a well-qualified and efficient staff complement through the provision of good remuneration packages, pleasant working conditions, recognition, excellent resources, efficient leadership and a friendly working environment.
